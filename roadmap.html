Roadmap

ELARIS is under heavy development.

This platform is moving. More modules are on the road, deeper hardware support is being built, and the next major integrations are already visible on the horizon.

Now

Sharpen the product core

  • Refine modules, help screens and commissioning flows.
  • Keep improving ESPHome installer and add-peripheral workflows.
  • Ship more module logic where real field installations need it.
Next

Expand hardware reach

  • Modbus integration.
  • More board templates, peripherals and validation.
  • Stronger field-oriented hardware flows for installers.
Under evaluation

Voice & AI layer

  • Alexa voice workflows for practical smart-home commands.
  • Google Home / Assistant direction for broader household control.
  • Microsoft Copilot-style workflows for summaries, diagnostics and assisted control ideas.
More modules on the road

We are not stopping at the current library.

Keep shipping new domain logic

Lighting, climate, water and energy are only the beginning of what ELARIS can grow into.

Broaden commercial fit

Every good new module makes the platform useful in more real installations, from villas to plant rooms to hospitality.

Stay coherent

The goal is not feature clutter. The goal is to keep one strong app language while the capability range grows.

Public signal

Say it clearly: development is active, practical and ongoing.

The site should communicate momentum. ELARIS is not a frozen static product. It is a serious local-first automation platform that is still expanding in modules, hardware flows and integrations.
