Use cases

Built for real installations, not just demos.

ELARIS fits best when one product needs to move from daily operation to engineering setup without changing tools.

Homes and villas

Lighting, heating, weather logic, scenes, blinds, recirculation and presence simulation in one interface.

  • Clean dashboard for daily use
  • Modules instead of hard-to-maintain rule spaghetti
  • Expandable with more boards and sensors over time

Boiler rooms and plant spaces

Hydronic logic, solar boiler control, pumps, recirculation and safety conditions with history and alarms.

  • Purpose-built temperature and relay logic
  • Local control without cloud dependency
  • Good fit for custom engineering jobs

Hotels and hospitality

Shared weather, irrigation, outdoor shading, pool logic and room-level or area-level control from one base system.

  • Visual supervision for staff
  • Modular expansion by area or subsystem
  • Scenes and schedules for repeatable operation

Gardens and irrigation

Pump, valve, leak and weather-aware irrigation logic with clean mapping between relays and field sensors.

  • Wind and outdoor sensors can live on separate nodes
  • Irrigation flows sit next to the rest of the building logic
  • Useful for villas, farms and landscaped properties
